Our Story

2010

Foundation

Founded in a small garage by tech enthusiasts Alex, Vivian, and Noah (AVN), we began with a vision to make cutting-edge technology accessible to everyone.

2013

First Breakthrough

Launched our award-winning AVN-X1 Smart TV, revolutionizing home entertainment with intuitive controls and unmatched picture quality.

2016

Global Expansion

Opened our first international offices in Tokyo and Berlin, establishing AVN as a global player in consumer electronics.

2019

Sustainability Initiative

Committed to carbon-neutral operations and launched our eco-friendly product line, reducing electronic waste by 40%.

2023

Present Day

With over 500 employees worldwide and millions of satisfied customers, we continue to push boundaries in smart home technology and AI integration.
